Khanna: Management Team

Meet the dedicated team behind Khaana's success. Our leadership and staff work together to deliver exceptional food service experiences, combining innovation, passion, and expertise in every meal we serve.



Employee Positions:

Founder & CEO: James Burkart

Chief Operating Officer (COO): Samantha Smith

Role Responsibilities:

Founder & CEO: Visionary leader who started Khaana to bring fresh, high-quality meals to the community, and oversees company strategy, partnerships, and long-term growth.

Chief Operating Officer (COO): Manages daily operations, supply chain logistics, and vendor relationships to ensure seamless delivery of food and services.

Chief Marketing Officer (CMO): Leads branding, advertising, and digital campaigns to grow Khaana’s customer base and strengthen its presence in the foodservice industry.

Chief Financial Officer (CFO): Handles budgeting, financial planning, and investment strategies to maintain profitability and support expansion.

Head of Culinary Innovation: Develops new recipes, seasonal menus, and sustainable sourcing strategies to keep Khaana’s offerings fresh and unique.

Human Resources Director: Oversees recruitment, training, and employee development, ensuring Khaana remains a great place to work.
